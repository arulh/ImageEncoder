Image Encoder

This application allows you to insert hidden messages securely within images using a technique called steganography. The encryption algorithm manipulates the lower order bits of desired pixels with the key such that the encrypted image is indistinguishable from the original. Higher resolution images conceal text even better. Simply upload an image with your desired text and you will get an encrypted image along with a key that can be used later on to decrypt the image.
